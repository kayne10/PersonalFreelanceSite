Computer Science at University of Colorado Boulder

I graduated from University of Colorado Boulder in the Spring of 2018. As a Computer Science major, I engaged in software development classes such as Algortihms, Operating Systems, and Big Data Architecture. I have a passion for Web Development and Deep/Machine learning.

Data Science at Galvanize

Afer graduating from the University of Colorado Boulder, I wanted to continue my education by pursuing a 15 week Data Science Immersive Program at Galvanaize in Denver, CO. This program was a Python-based curriculum focused on machine learning, statistical analysis and big data. I came out with a huge background in machine learning and statistical analysis where I strongly developed data visualization skills as well. In addition to participating in many case studies and projects, I spent my last two intense weeks focused on my capstone project that consisted of a beer recommendation system and convolutional neural network classifying on beer style.

Full Stack Web Developing

After completing web development courses and projects on the side of all my studies, I am offering freelancing services for anyone interesting in needing a website. These services target anyone such as Skiers, Restaraunt Managers, Public Announcements, and other freelancers with a business. The number of pages is not an issue, I develope static and dynamic websites that suits any desired requirements. Sometimes, Front-End development can get boring to me, so I started to focus on best strategies for Back-End/Full-Stack development. My favorite frameworks are Django/Flask, Node/Express, and Rails.
