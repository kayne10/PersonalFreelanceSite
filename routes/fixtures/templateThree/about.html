About the Garage Sale

At the Garage Sale, there will be new items available for purchase almost every month. All items at the Garage Sale are kept in best condition possible. You must sign up or register a new account in order to participate in any transactions. All accounts are free to use. All purchases and sales are final. There are no refunds. Please Enjoy the Garage Sale!!
